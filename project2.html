Kimchi Fried Rice

What is it?

Kimchi Fried Rice is one of the most popular dish among the Koreans due to its simplicity and cheapness. People who cannot afford expensive meals favor kimchi fried rice because it is so cheap. Also, it is simple to make because people can utilize leftover rice from the last meal to mix it with a wide variety of ingredients ranging from spam, beef, chicken, bacon, and even shrimp.

Ingredients
Ingredients Quantity
roughly chopped Kimchi 6 ounces
medium diced onions 1/2 of an onion
Cooked Rice 2 cups
Kimchi Juice 2 tablespoons
Soy Sauce 2 teaspoons
Sesame Oil 2 teaspoons
Eggs 2
small-diced Spam, or leftover cooked meat 1/2 cup
